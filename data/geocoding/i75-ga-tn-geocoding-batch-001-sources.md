One row per listing: source, verification method, confidence. Coordinates were
researched from public, no-key sources (brand location data, official USDOT/state
datasets, OpenStreetMap-derived POI data, coordinate-publishing directories) and
then 55 of 139 rows were re-verified by an independent adversarial audit pass
(verdicts appear as `[AUDITED:…]` in the CSV notes and are summarized in the
review report). Direct geocoder APIs (Nominatim/Census) were unreachable from the
research environment; nothing was scraped in violation of service terms and no
paid APIs or API keys were used. Coordinates that could not be confidently
verified were left blank rather than guessed.
