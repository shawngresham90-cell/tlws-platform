# Batch 48 — I-80 Wyoming (Evanston/UT line → Rock Springs → Rawlins → Laramie → Cheyenne → Pine Bluffs/NE line): Source Report

Every listing was web-verified during research on **2026-07-15**. No field was invented: anything a source did not state is left blank in the CSV. Coordinates are blank on every row (geocoding is a separate verified workflow). Research method: web search against official brand/operator locators (Love's, Pilot/Flying J, TA/Petro, Sinclair, CAT Scale, state DOT/port-of-entry) as primary sources, with directory/review sites (iExit, Allstays, TruckMap, Find Truck Service, truckstopsandservices.com, coopsareopen.com, state rest-area guides) as secondary confirmation.

- Major high-elevation freight corridor with severe winter weather; WYDOT has expanded I-80 truck parking under its Winter Freight project (Fort Bridger, Fort Steele, Quealy Dome, Summit, Pine Bluffs).
- Cross-segment reconciliation at **Rawlins (Exits 209/214)**: TA Rawlins, Flying J #763, and the Rawlins I-80 weigh station were returned by both researchers (identical names, auto-deduped once); the Flying J #763 CAT scale (differently named across segments) was kept once and its East duplicate dropped before compile.
- The Cheyenne I-80/I-25 interchange cluster (Flying J #759, Love's #220, Blue Beacon) is signed as I-25 Exit 7 but serves I-80 freight; those rows carry a blank I-80 exit_number and say so in their descriptions, rather than assert an unverified I-80 exit.
- Only WYDOT truck-parking/rest areas with clearly documented facilities were included; smaller un-detailed pull-offs were not padded. Love's Rock Springs '#603/Exit 102' surfaced in one aggregator but is not on the official Love's locator, so it was excluded as unverified.
