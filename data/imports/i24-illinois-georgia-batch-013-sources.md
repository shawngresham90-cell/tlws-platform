- **Pilot Travel Center (Rising Fawn, GA area)** — Serves I-59 Exit 4 (Puddin Ridge Rd) near Rising Fawn in southern Dade County — on I-59, roughly 16 miles from the I-24 Georgia segment; not at I-24 exits 167/169. _(from seg-georgia.json)_
- **Motel 6 Metropolis** — Located near I-24 Exit 37 (2119 E 5th St) but its parking is described as convenient for cars and small trucks only; no semi/large-vehicle truck parking confirmed. _(from seg-il-east.json)_
- **Baymont by Wyndham Metropolis** — Located at 203 E Front St on the Metropolis riverfront roughly 2.6 miles from I-24, in the casino district; not tied to the I-24 exit and no truck parking confirmed. _(from seg-il-east.json)_
- **Harrah's Metropolis (casino riverfront area)** — Riverfront casino district facilities are far from I-24 Exit 37 and not tied to the interstate corridor; explicitly out of scope. _(from seg-il-east.json)_
- **I-24 Illinois weigh station** — No weigh station exists on I-24 in Illinois per statewide weigh station listings; the nearest state scales are on I-57 at Marion, outside this segment. _(from seg-il-east.json)_
- **Exit 27 (New Columbia / Big Bay Rd) services** — iExit and AARoads confirm Exit 27 is a rural diamond interchange with 0 gas stations, 0 restaurants, and 0 hotels; no trucker facilities exist there. _(from seg-il-east.json)_
- **Britton's Wrecker Service Inc** — Real 24/7 heavy-duty towing/truck repair facility (6685 US Hwy 45 N, Vienna), but its own site places it 4.5 miles north of I-24 on US-45 - outside the at-exit corridor scope per segment rules. _(from seg-il-west.json)_
- **Fort Massac Rest Area** — Only rest area on I-24 in Illinois; located at mile marker 37 near Metropolis - outside this segment's mile 0-20 range (belongs to the eastern segment). _(from seg-il-west.json)_
- **Illinois weigh stations near I-24/I-57 junction (Marion NB/SB)** — No weigh station exists on I-24 within mile 0-20 in Illinois; the nearby scales are on I-57 at ~MM 47 near Marion, north of the I-24 junction - a different interstate outside this corridor. _(from seg-il-west.json)_
- **Big Bay / New Columbia exit businesses** — Candidate list placed Big Bay/New Columbia at exit 16, but iExit shows this interchange is actually Exit 27 - beyond the mile 20 cutoff for this segment. _(from seg-il-west.json)_
- **Casey's (306 N 1st St, Vienna)** — In-town convenience store with diesel roughly 2 miles from I-24 via US-45/IL-146; no source documents truck parking, truck lanes, or other truck amenities, and it is not on Casey's designated truck-stop list. _(from seg-il-west.json)_
- **H & H Fuel (Goreville)** — Small-town gas station about 3 miles from I-24 Exit 7 in downtown Goreville; no evidence of truck amenities and not at the exit. _(from seg-il-west.json)_
