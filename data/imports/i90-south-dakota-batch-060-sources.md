# Batch 60 — I-90 South Dakota (WY line/Spearfish → Rapid City → Wall → Murdo → Chamberlain → Mitchell → Sioux Falls → MN line/Valley Springs): Source Report

Every listing was web-verified during research on **2026-07-15**. No field was invented: anything a source did not state is left blank in the CSV. Coordinates are blank on every row (geocoding is a separate verified workflow). Research method: web search against official brand/operator locators (Love's, Pilot/Flying J, TA/Petro, Sinclair, CAT Scale, state DOT/port-of-entry) as primary sources, with directory/review sites (iExit, Allstays, TruckMap, Find Truck Service, truckstopsandservices.com, coopsareopen.com, state rest-area guides) as secondary confirmation.

- Longest I-90 state stretch (~412 mi). West: Spearfish/Sturgis/Rapid City/Box Elder/Wall/Kadoka. Central: Murdo/Vivian/Presho/Oacoma-Chamberlain/Plankinton/Mitchell. East: Mitchell/Hartford/Sioux Falls/Brandon/Valley Springs.
- Cross-segment reconciliation: the Kadoka Discount Fuel (Sinclair, Exit 150) was returned by both West and Central — kept once (West, enriched). The Mitchell cluster (Cubby's Cenex Exit 330; I-90 Travel Center Exit 332; its CAT Scale) was returned by both Central and East — each kept once (Central). The distinct co-located I-90 Travel Center truck wash, Larry's I-90 Service, and Super 8 Mitchell were kept in East.
- Only three named CAT Scales corridor-wide were duplicated across research (Bosselman Rapid City, Flying J Rapid City, Love's Box Elder in West; Pilot Murdo + I-90 TC Mitchell in Central; Love's + Flying J Sioux Falls in East) — total 7 distinct CAT scales, each kept once.
- Ports of entry: Tilford (EB, ~MP 39, West) and Valley Springs (Exit 410, MN line, East). No central-segment POE exists. The Flying J #716, its CAT Scale, and Jim & Ron's sit at I-29 Exit 83 at the I-90/I-29 junction serving I-90 traffic (exit labeled 'I-29 Exit 83', interstate kept I-90, junction noted in-description).
- Rest areas/welcome centers with no town assigned the nearest named I-90 locality (Spearfish/Wasta/Belvidere/Vivian/Chamberlain/White Lake/Salem/Valley Springs); milepost-only sites left exit blank with MM noted in-description.
- Omitted, not fabricated: no verifiable TA/Petro on SD I-90 (the Exit 399 Cliff Ave facility is a Love's, not a TA); Sioux Falls stops deep on I-29/I-229 (off the I-90 mainline); Alexandria, Kennebec and small-town gas stations without verifiable truck parking; the permanently-closed Tilford information center.
