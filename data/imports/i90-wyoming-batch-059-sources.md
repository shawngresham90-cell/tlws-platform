# Batch 59 — I-90 Wyoming (MT line/Ranchester → Sheridan → Buffalo → Gillette → Moorcroft → Sundance → SD line/Beulah): Source Report

Every listing was web-verified during research on **2026-07-15**. No field was invented: anything a source did not state is left blank in the CSV. Coordinates are blank on every row (geocoding is a separate verified workflow). Research method: web search against official brand/operator locators (Love's, Pilot/Flying J, TA/Petro, Sinclair, CAT Scale, state DOT/port-of-entry) as primary sources, with directory/review sites (iExit, Allstays, TruckMap, Find Truck Service, truckstopsandservices.com, coopsareopen.com, state rest-area guides) as secondary confirmation.

- Short NE-corner corridor (~207 mi). Separate from the I-80 Wyoming draft (Batch 48), which covers the southern cities (Evanston/Rock Springs/Rawlins/Cheyenne) — no city overlap, so no dedup collision.
- Cross-segment reconciliation: the Love's Travel Stop #965 (Buffalo Exit 58) and its co-located CAT Scale #3528 were returned by both West and East research (under differently-normalized names); each kept once in the West part. Red Eagle Food Store (Buffalo) is retained in East; the Buffalo Cenex Zip Trip #60 sits at the I-90/I-25 junction (exit left blank, junction noted in-description).
- Only two CAT Scales verified on the corridor (Love's Buffalo #3528, Flying J Gillette #762). A directory claim of a CAT Scale at Common Cents Sheridan was NOT corroborated by the CAT locator and was omitted rather than fabricated.
- Wyoming ports of entry: Sheridan POE (~mile 16) and Sundance POE (Exit 189, westbound). No separate Beulah/SD-line POE building could be verified; the Beulah entry is the Travel Wyoming welcome center. Rest areas with no town were assigned the nearest named I-90 locality (Sheridan/Moorcroft/Sundance/Beulah).
- Omitted, not fabricated: no verifiable commercial truck wash on the corridor (the '307 Truck Wash' hit was in Casper, off-corridor); Ranchester Conoco not confirmed as a parking truck stop; several Gillette oilfield tire/towing outfits lacked verifiable address+phone and were left out.
