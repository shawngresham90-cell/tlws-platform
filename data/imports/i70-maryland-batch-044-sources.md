# Batch 44 — I-70 Maryland: Source Report

Every listing was verified against public sources during research on **2026-07-15**. No field was invented: anything a source did not state is left blank in the CSV. Coordinates are blank on every row (geocoding is a separate verified workflow). Research method: web search against official brand/operator locators (Pilot/Flying J, STTC, Butler Tire, Maryland State Police, MDOT SHA, PA Turnpike, CAT Scale) as primary sources, with directory/review sites (iExit, TruckMap, Roadnow, coopsareopen.com, VisitMaryland/VisitFrederick) as secondary confirmation, across the I-70 crossing of Maryland in two segments: West (the Big Pool/Clear Spring area and Hagerstown at the I-68/I-81 interchanges, then South Mountain) and East (New Market/Mount Airy through the Frederick corridor to the I-70 eastern terminus at I-695 near Baltimore). The Frederick-to-Baltimore stretch is genuinely sparse on branded travel centers, so tire/road-service operators that cover the I-70 corridor are included there. Love's Hagerstown (on I-81 Exit 10A, not I-70) and the Hancock Truck Plaza (conflicting 'closed' signals) were omitted rather than guessed.
