# Batch 54 — I-80 Pennsylvania (Ohio line/Sharon → Clarion → DuBois → Bellefonte → Milton → Bloomsburg → Hazleton → Stroudsburg/Delaware Water Gap/NJ line): Source Report

Every listing was web-verified during research on **2026-07-15**. No field was invented: anything a source did not state is left blank in the CSV. Coordinates are blank on every row (geocoding is a separate verified workflow). Research method: web search against official brand/operator locators (Love's, Pilot/Flying J, TA/Petro, Sinclair, CAT Scale, state DOT/port-of-entry) as primary sources, with directory/review sites (iExit, Allstays, TruckMap, Find Truck Service, truckstopsandservices.com, coopsareopen.com, state rest-area guides) as secondary confirmation.

- Long free-interstate corridor across northern PA (~310 mi). Live PA production rows: 0 (the separate I-70 PA batch is a draft, not merged), so no dedup avoid-list is required and there is no city overlap with I-70 PA anyway.
- Cross-segment reconciliation: the DuBois Exit 97 anchor (Pilot #336, Sheetz Falls Creek, and the Pilot CAT scale) was returned by both West and Central — kept once (West). The Bloomsburg Exit 232 / Mifflinville Exit 242 anchor (TA Bloomsburg, Love's #324, their CAT scales and tire care) was returned by both Central and East — kept once (East). Central retains only its unique middle rows (Clearfield Exit 120 → Milton Exit 215).
- The generic Mill Hall Pilot was renamed 'Pilot Travel Center - Mill Hall' for a distinct name/slug. The three PennDOT rest areas keyed by mile marker (146/194/220) carry blank exit numbers and were assigned their nearest named I-80 locality (Snow Shoe / Mill Hall / Danville) to satisfy the required city field.
- Omitted, not fabricated: Hazleton-area facilities on I-81 (not I-80); a 'Love's Clearfield' and 'Love's Milton' that were actually Sapp Bros and Flying J #555 respectively; the Delaware Water Gap Welcome Center (closed for building damage, reopening unconfirmed); legacy Mifflinville truck stops of unverified current operation.
